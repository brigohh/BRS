SITE OVERVIEW

Welcome to BRS

BRS (Bridget Review System) is a rebuild of the GRS officiating review tool for logging, checking, and publishing play reviews, with new pages and a reworked workflow. Here's a tour of what's here and how it compares to GRS. Click any card to jump straight to that page.
Note: this page is a temporary internal summary and won't be part of the live site.
Brand new pages
RIM NEW
rim.html
Open page
RIM (Review Insights and Messaging) is the home base for everything outside of game review itself.
  • Weekly schedule view with an expandable full month calendar, games, Summer Project days, and off days all marked
  • One place to jump into Interactions, Threads, Message Board, Playlists, Assessments, and Points of Education
  • Personalized landing cards greeting each reviewer by name
Published Games NEW
published-games.html
Open page
A dedicated, searchable archive of every game that's been fully second-checked and published.
  • Browse and search all published games in one grid, independent of the working queue
  • See who second-checked each game and when it was published at a glance
  • Quick access back into any past game's full review
Assessments NEW
assessments.html
Open page
A knowledge-check quiz tool for reviewers.
  • 10-question quizzes on a monthly, 1-week-window cadence
  • Answers stay hidden mid-quiz so reviewers can't peek ahead
  • Manager-side filtering and flagging to track who needs follow-up
Comment Library NEW
comment-library.html
Open page
A searchable bank of pre-written comments by infraction type, used to speed up writing reviews in the Review tab.
  • Comments organized by infraction and rating so the right note is one click away
  • Uses the same {CP}/{DP} placeholder convention as the GRS auto-comments sheet, so existing language carries over
Playlists NEW
playlists.html
Open page
A video library for grouping and rewatching clips, by play type, by rating, or curated by hand.
  • Spotify-style browse grid with curated, by-play-type, and by-rating sections
  • Queue + video + play info side by side in the detail view
Points of Education NEW
poe.html
Open page
A reference page for league-wide points of emphasis for the season, surfaced directly inside the review workflow rather than living in a separate memo.
  • Centralized list of current points of emphasis, linked from RIM
Reworked from GRS
Review
review.html
Open page
Where individual plays get logged and rated, rebuilt as a full possession-by-possession workflow instead of a flat entry form.
GRSA basic first-pass logging form: enter a call, pick a rating, move to the next play.
BRSA full 40-possession queue with a dedicated rating form, comment library suggestions tied to infraction type, a "Help / Side Assist" panel, video synced to each play, one-click Slack and League Ops messaging, and a rating/comment mismatch warning so a written note can't contradict the selected grade. Shooting-foul events now auto-fill sensible defaults instead of starting blank.
Second Check
second-check.html
Open page
Where a second reviewer verifies the first pass before a game is published.
GRSA single review list with limited filtering and no dedicated views for special categories of plays.
BRSSplit into dedicated tabs (Second Check, PTIW, OIW, Important Calls), each with its own filtered table. Adds a vertical play-by-play timeline with progress tracking, a wider synced video panel, note bubbles for leaving feedback on specific plays, an accountability flow with pass/fail grading and manager check-ins, and a manager roster view for oversight across reviewers.
