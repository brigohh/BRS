How Assessments Work
Assessments are auto-generated every month — 10 questions based on your last 15–20 games. Each assessment tests your infraction rating accuracy and ref coverage judgment using real plays from your reviews. Pass with 70%+ to complete — below that, you'll take a focused retake on areas you missed. Assessments are open for 1 week, and the correct answers are only revealed in your final score so you can retake without memorizing them.
